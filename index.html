The Grammar School at Leeds

Places On Campus

Swimming Pool

The 25 meter-long pool is used by both junior and senior students. With diving blocks, changing rooms and a spectator area, this state-of-the-art pool is a fantastic place.


Library

The School Library is a giant, 2 floor room full of all sorts of books. There are also tablesfor studying and a room in which you can chill with your friends or get advice from the sixth-formers.


3G pitches

The 3G pitches are use for both boys' and girls's rugby as well as the numerous rugby matches held throughout the season.
